# Annotated source register

**All URLs accessed 21 July 2026.** This register records what was verified, how, and — at least as
importantly — **what was not**. A source listed here is not automatically safe to quote: read the
verification column first.

**Verification levels**

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **PRIMARY** | The primary document was fetched and read; quotations are verbatim |
| **PRIMARY-PARTIAL** | Primary document fetched, but only part read (paywall, preview, extraction limit) |
| **SECONDARY** | Verified through a reliable secondary source only |
| **METADATA** | Existence, title, date and identifier confirmed; content not read |
| **NOT VERIFIED** | Could not be confirmed. **Do not cite** |

**Classification**: SETTLED LAW · CASE LAW · REGULATOR GUIDANCE · CONSULTATION/PROPOSAL · STANDARD ·
DEFINITION · RESEARCH FINDING · COMMERCIAL FACT · INCIDENT · UNRESOLVED

**Two corrections this research produced that contradict common belief**

- **UK corporate directors are not banned.** As at 21 July 2026 only the exception-making power is
  commenced; s.155 (one natural-person director) is the live rule.
- **SR 11-7 is no longer in force**, superseded 17 April 2026 — and its successor **expressly excludes
  generative and agentic AI from scope**.
